Attendees select the sessions of their choice in order to track an Award recipient, track a Baldrige Category, industry sector, or mix and match. Sessions include a presentation related to the Baldrige Criteria, as noted below. Organizational results are addressed along with the appropriate Criteria Category describing the approaches and deployment.


The four 2012 Baldrige Award recipients are: Lockheed Martin Missiles and Fire Control, Grand Prairie, TX. (manufacturing); MESA Products Inc., Tulsa, Okla. (small business); North Mississippi Health Services, Tupelo, Miss. (health care); and City of Irving, TX (nonprofit).


Former Baldrige Award recipients presenting on other topics are: Advocate Good Samaritan Hospital (health care); Iredell-Statesville Schools (education); MidwayUSA (small business); Poudre Valley Health System (health care); Premier Inc. (service); Schneck Medical Center (health care); Southcentral Foundation (health care); and University of Wisconsin-Stout (education).
